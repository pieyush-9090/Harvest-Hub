Our Story

Connecting farms to families since 2022

Welcome to HarvestHub

We're on a mission to revolutionize how people access fresh, organic produce by creating a direct connection between local farmers and conscious consumers.

10+ Years of Excellence
200+ Partner Farms
50k+ Happy Customers

2022

The Beginning

HarvestHub was founded by Michael and Sarah Chen, who left their corporate jobs to pursue their passion for sustainable agriculture and food systems.

2023

Growing Network

We expanded our network to include over 50 family farms across the region, focusing on organic certification and sustainable farming practices.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launched our e-commerce platform to make fresh produce more accessible to urban communities, with same-day delivery options.

2024

Subscription Model

Introduced our popular subscription boxes, allowing customers to receive curated seasonal produce delivered to their doorstep weekly.

2024

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our zero-waste packaging program and carbon-neutral delivery fleet, reducing our environmental footprint by 60%.

Today

Looking Forward

With over 200 partner farms and 50,000 customers, we continue to innovate and expand our mission of connecting people with fresh, sustainable food.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by principles that prioritize sustainability, community, and quality

Sustainability

We champion farming practices that protect and regenerate our environment, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.

Community

We build meaningful connections between farmers and consumers, fostering a transparent and supportive food ecosystem.

Quality

We never compromise on the freshness, flavor, and nutritional value of our products, delivering excellence in every harvest.

Fair Trade

We ensure farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work, supporting rural economies and sustainable livelihoods.
